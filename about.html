about

ceo profile

Meet Naa Oblitey, the founder and CEO of Cocktail Hub Gh, a company that provides innovative solutions for the hospitality industry. Naa has over 10 years of experience in the field, having worked as a consultant, and trainer for various hotels, restaurants, and bars. She is passionate about creating value for her clients and delivering exceptional service to their customers. Naa is also a certified bar tender, having completed the International Bartender Course in 2023. She enjoys mixing drinks and creating new recipes, as well as sharing her knowledge and skills with others. She believes that being a bar tender is not only a fun and rewarding profession, but also a great way to connect with people and understand their needs and preferences. Naa is a visionary leader who inspires her team to achieve excellence and innovation. She is always looking for new opportunities and challenges to grow her business and make a positive impact on the world. She is also a generous and caring person who supports various social causes and charities. When she is not working or bartending, Naa likes to spend time with her family and friends, travel to new places, read books, and watch documentaries. She is always eager to learn new things and explore new cultures. She is fluent in English. J Naa is proud to be the CEO of Cocktail Hub Gh, and She looks forward to serving you with her expertise and enthusiasm.

our mission
